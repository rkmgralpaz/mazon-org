Thank you for your dedication to challah baking, creating change and building community! Congratulations to all of our chapters for your hard work and effort to bake a difference in the world


Please fill out this donation report by January 31st, 2024 so we can process the donations you collected this semester. There are also some questions to help MAZON understand how your chapter functioned and what support you need for Spring 2024. 

 

As a reminder, the funds you raised as a chapter on the Classy platform will be split as follows:

  • 80% donated to a single  local anti-hunger 501(c)(3) of your choice (your chapter's local partner)

  • 20% donated to MAZON, earmarked only for the Challah for Hunger program (funding annual chapter stipends, leadership development, etc.) and college hunger initiatives.


If you raised any cash donations this semester:

  • You are welcome to hold onto those funds to use at your discretion, e.g. purchasing ingredients, materials, other resources for bakes/events, etc. We also know many chapters partner for select bakes with other student groups, and direct some of the funds to other mutually chosen causes; you may use your cash funds to donate to those organizations. MAZON will still be responsible for directing 80% of your Classy-collected funds to your local anti-hunger partner.


  • If you have cash funds you would like to be reflected as part of your Classy total, and thus be split 80/20 as described above, please deposit the cash by Jan 15th, 2024. You may:

    • Complete one transaction in Classy to your chapter page. Don’t have a Classy page yet? Set one up here; or

    • Send a check to MAZON. Checks can be made out to MAZON: A Jewish Response to Hunger. Please include “[School Name] Challah bake proceeds” in the memo line, and a note including contact information for at least one board member or faculty/staff advisor. Send checks to: Attn: Naama Haviv, MAZON: A Jewish Response to Hunger, 15303 Ventura Blvd. #1100, Sherman Oaks CA 91403


As always, contact c4h@mazon.org with questions.
